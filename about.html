About

Hi, I'm Fejiro.
Nice to meet you.

I'm a Toronto-based product designer with a background that bridges two worlds — information technology and human-centred design. That combination is intentional. It means I can collaborate with developers, understand technical constraints, and still put the user experience first.

I got into UX design because I genuinely care about how things feel to the people using them. Before I ever open Figma, I'm watching, listening, and asking questions. I've conducted research interviews across Canada, the UK, India, and South Korea. I've sat in parking lots observing car wash users. I've tested betting flows with first-time users. The fieldwork is what drives the design.

My IT background gives me an edge most designers don't have — I can read code, understand implementation constraints, and have real conversations with engineers. That means fewer handoff surprises and better outcomes for users.

Right now I'm open to freelance projects, collaborations, and full-time roles. If you have a problem worth solving, I'd love to hear about it.
